Lewis Combes' website

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Sheffield, under the supervision of Haluk Şengün. My research is in number theory, in particular the study of Bianchi modular forms, cohomology of arithmetic groups, and Selmer groups.

My contact information can be found on the University of Sheffield website.

My most recent complete piece of work is my Masters thesis: Mathematics of Bitcoin: The ECDSA (University of Warwick, 2018).
